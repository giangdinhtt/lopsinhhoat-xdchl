Lam theo huong dan sau de bang diem cua phan doi minh tu dong cap nhat:
    1. Chay file install.bat truoc khi tao worksheet moi
    2. Vao file bang diem cua cac doi, click chon New worksheet, khi co cua so hien ra, click chon "Don't Update"
    3. Cac thong tin can thiet se duoc dien day du, cac phan doi truong chi viec dien thong tin thi dua cua cac thanh vien trong doi la du, diem so se duoc tu dong cap nhat
